About Us

EcoVerse is a free platform that turns everyday environmental actions into a shared community movement — because small steps create big change

About EcoVerse

We are a student-founded NGO from Albania on a mission to make environmental protection simple, fun, and social. Through our app, anyone can join local eco-actions, earn rewards, and be part of a growing green community.

We Take Action

From collecting trash to planting trees — our users don't just read about climate change, they do something about it. Every action counts.

We Build Community

EcoVerse connects like-minded people through group events and shared challenges. You're never alone in making a difference.

We Make It Fun

Points, badges, leaderboards and rewards keep motivation high. Doing good for the planet has never felt this rewarding.

Our Goals

From a small community in Albania to a regional movement — here is where EcoVerse is headed.

Launch the App

Release the EcoVerse beta and run our first local environmental actions in Albania.

Build a Community

Grow a founding group of 25–50 active members who share a passion for the environment.

Expand to Cities

Build active EcoVerse communities across multiple Albanian cities within 3 years.

Partner with NGOs

Collaborate with other environmental organizations across Albania and beyond.

Supermarket Partnerships

Work with local supermarkets to offer donation options and real-world rewards.

Go Regional

Expand EcoVerse into neighboring countries and collect over 1 tonne of waste together.
